# Euler characteristic profiles

Euler characteristic profiles summarize topology by sampling the Euler characteristic over filtration parameter values.

In the paper:

- Input filtration: multiparameter directional filtrations, including [[Principal-component multifiltration]].
- Sampling: 7 by 7 by 7 grid.
- Model use: standalone ECP and combined [[PH plus ECP descriptor]].

## Empirical Role

ECP is usually the strongest standalone topological descriptor family in the reported results.
