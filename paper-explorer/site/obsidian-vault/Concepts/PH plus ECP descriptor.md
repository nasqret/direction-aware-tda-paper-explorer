# PH plus ECP descriptor

The PH+ECP descriptor concatenates features from [[Persistent homology]] and [[Euler characteristic profiles]].

## Role In Paper

It is the most important combined topological representation. Directional PH+ECP reaches:

- RTPxz: R2 0.978, MAE 1.86.
- RTPxy: R2 0.938, MAE 1.69.
- TD: R2 0.836, MAE 1.53.
- ATTD: R2 0.825, MAE 2.06.
