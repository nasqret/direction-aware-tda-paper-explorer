# RTPxz

RTPxz uses [[Random Trigonometric Phase dataset]] structures evaluated along x and z directions.

## Regime

Strongly anisotropic. The x direction is mechanically easier, while the z direction is the hard axis.

## Reported Summary

- sigma(k): 0.40.
- sigma(L): 1.89.
- CNN: R2 0.985, MAE 1.62.
- Directional PH+ECP: R2 0.978, MAE 1.86.
- Non-directional PH+ECP: R2 0.754, MAE 7.05.

## Importance

This is the clearest demonstration that [[Direction-aware TDA]] recovers information lost by non-directional descriptors.
