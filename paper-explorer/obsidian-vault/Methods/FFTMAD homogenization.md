# FFTMAD homogenization

FFTMAD is used to compute effective Young's modulus under uniaxial compression.

## Role

It provides the target values for [[Young's modulus prediction]].

## Material Parameters In Paper

- RTP: Au-Ag-like bulk material, Young's modulus 81.5 GPa, Poisson ratio 0.39.
- TD and ATTD: aluminium-like bulk material, Young's modulus 70.0 GPa, Poisson ratio 0.33.
