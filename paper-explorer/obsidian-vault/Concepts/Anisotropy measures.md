# Anisotropy measures

The paper uses two anisotropy summaries computed from binary voxelized structures.

## Spectral Measure

The spectral measure uses directional second moments of the Fourier power spectrum. Larger spread across axes indicates stronger directional structure.

## Correlation-Length Measure

The real-space measure integrates directional autocorrelation functions up to the first zero crossing.

## Result Connection

Directional descriptor gains increase most clearly when anisotropy is large, especially in [[RTPxz]].
