# Persistent homology

Persistent homology tracks connected components, loops, and voids across a filtration.

In the paper:

- Input filtration: [[Cone-based filtration]].
- Complex type: 3D cubical complex.
- Homology dimensions: 0, 1, and 2.
- Vectorization: persistence images with a 10 by 10 grid.
- Model use: standalone PH and combined [[PH plus ECP descriptor]].
