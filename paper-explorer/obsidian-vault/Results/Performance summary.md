# Performance summary

## Main Pattern

Directional descriptors outperform non-directional descriptors when anisotropy matters.

## Strongest Case

[[RTPxz]] directional PH+ECP reaches R2 0.978, close to the CNN baseline R2 0.985. Non-directional PH+ECP reaches only R2 0.754.

## Generalization Case

[[Anisotropic Transformed TD dataset]] shows the same qualitative pattern outside the RTP generation mechanism.

## Weak Anisotropy

[[RTPxy]] and [[Topologically Diverse dataset]] show that directional descriptors remain competitive even when the tested directions are close to isotropic.
