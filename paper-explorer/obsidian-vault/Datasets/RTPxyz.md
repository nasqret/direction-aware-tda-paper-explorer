# RTPxyz

RTPxyz aggregates all three compression directions for [[Random Trigonometric Phase dataset]] structures.

## Source

Supplementary Information, Section 2.

## Reported Summary

- sigma(k): 0.38.
- sigma(L): 1.77.
- CNN: R2 0.990, MAE 1.88.
- Directional PH+ECP: R2 0.974, MAE 2.89.
- Non-directional PH+ECP: R2 0.511, MAE 9.94.

## Interpretation

Non-directional predictions form separate branches for easy and hard axes because the descriptor cannot encode loading direction.
