# Random Trigonometric Phase dataset

The RTP dataset is generated from a periodic random trigonometric scalar field and thresholded into binary porous structures.

## Parameters In Paper

- Grid: 80 by 80 by 80.
- Number of structures: 500.
- Trigonometric modes: K sampled from 10 to 30.
- Porosity target: uniform from 0.2 to 0.8.
- Anisotropy scaling: $s_x=s_y=1$, $s_z=0.2$ for elongated structures.
