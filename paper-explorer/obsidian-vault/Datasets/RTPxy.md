# RTPxy

RTPxy uses [[Random Trigonometric Phase dataset]] structures evaluated along the x and y directions.

## Regime

Weakly anisotropic within the sampled axes.

## Reported Summary

- sigma(k): 0.11.
- sigma(L): 0.21.
- CNN: R2 0.979, MAE 1.02.
- Directional PH+ECP: R2 0.938, MAE 1.69.
- Non-directional PH+ECP: R2 0.916, MAE 1.98.
